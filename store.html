Important legal notice about digital files / blueprints

We will not list, host, or distribute digital files, blueprints, or instructions that enable manufacture of unserialized firearms. Creating or sharing such materials can be illegal and unsafe. If you were hoping to find "ghost gun" prints here — we cannot help with that.

Safer, legal alternatives we offer
  • Serialized receivers and complete pistols sold through lawful transfer to a licensed FFL.
  • Accessories: holsters, magazines (where legal), sights, cases, and maintenance gear.
  • Assistance arranging transfer to a local FFL for background checks and Form 4473 paperwork.

If you need parts or accessories listed for sale here, we can add them — all will follow applicable laws and transfer requirements.

How transfers work

  1. Place a deposit or request a quote online.
  2. We ship to your local FFL or you provide an FFL of your choice.
  3. Complete required ID and background check at the FFL before pickup.

By law we cannot ship firearms to private individuals in jurisdictions that require FFL transfers. You must present valid ID and pass a background check at the FFL transfer location.
